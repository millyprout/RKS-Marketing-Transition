Brand Essence

A permanent reference for what RKS should communicate and how it should feel.

Brand roleExperienced innovation partner

RKS helps organizations turn complex opportunities into products and experiences people value.

Brand characterHuman, intelligent, inventive, credible

Communications should feel informed and optimistic without becoming vague, inflated, or overly academic.

Messaging Principles

Use these principles across web, social, PR, case studies, and presentations.

  • Make the client problem and intended outcome clear.
  • Connect human understanding to business and product decisions.
  • Use specific evidence instead of broad claims.
  • Explain complex methods in approachable language.
  • Balance confidence with clarity; avoid jargon that does not add meaning.

Voice & Writing

RKS writing should sound expert, practical, and grounded.

UseClear and evidence-led

Concrete examples, active language, useful explanation, and an informed point of view.

AvoidGeneric innovation language

Empty superlatives, unexplained process language, inflated claims, and copy that could describe any design firm.

Visual Direction

The identity guidelines make one idea clear: the work should be carried by exceptional imagery, disciplined composition, and concise language.

  • Show less, but show it better.
  • Say less, but say it better.
  • Use professional, high-resolution photography.
  • Choose intentional light, angle, crop, and color.
  • Keep image treatment consistent across a series.
  • Let products, materials, and outcomes remain the visual focus.

Proof & Content

The strongest RKS marketing connects a claim to visible evidence.

  • Case studies and visual essays
  • Project outcomes and measurable impact
  • Founder and team expertise
  • Process and methodology
  • Awards, press, and long-term track record
