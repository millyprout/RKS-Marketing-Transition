Company Overview

This module is intended to preserve the full working context Helena needs before the live discussion.

What RKS Design doesHuman-centered product development

RKS combines research, strategy, industrial design, engineering, and development to create products, services, and experiences that people understand, adopt, and value.

Company historyMore than four decades of practice

RKS has built its approach through 45+ years of product-development work and the development of Psycho-Aesthetics® as a human-centered framework.

Mission and philosophyDesign for meaningful human response

The company connects human needs and perception with business viability, technical feasibility, and long-term market value.

Role of marketingTranslate expertise into visibility and opportunity

Marketing communicates RKS capabilities, demonstrates proof, supports credibility, and helps prospective clients understand when and why to engage the firm.

Primary services

Use the current RKS website service pages as the authoritative source for service language. The broader offering includes research, strategy, innovation, industrial design, engineering, development, and related product-development support.

Industries served

Use the current website and project portfolio to understand industry coverage. Healthcare and medical, consumer, technology, and commercial product-development work are especially visible in the portfolio.

Positioning & Design Philosophy

  • RKS is not simply an industrial design studio. It is an integrated innovation and product-development partner.
  • Human-centered design is tied to business viability, engineering feasibility, adoption, and measurable outcomes.
  • Psycho-Aesthetics® is an important foundation, but content should explain what it enables rather than relying on the name alone.
  • RKS communications should be specific. Use projects, decisions, methods, and outcomes to make expertise tangible.

Core Positioning

Human-Centered Product Design & Innovation

A human-centered product design and innovation firm that combines strategy, design, engineering, and Psycho-Aesthetics® to create products people love and businesses succeed with.

Core marketing pillars

  • Website & SEO: technical SEO, blog SEO, keyword research, content publishing, and Google Search Console.
  • Content marketing: blogs, visual essays, case studies, thought leadership, social assets, and reusable templates.
  • Social media: project stories, expertise, company news, video, blogs, and native posts.
  • Newsletter: monthly communication and process improvement.
  • PR & earned media: press, awards, interviews, outreach, and coordinated website support.
  • Video: interviews, project storytelling, studio capture, editing, and distribution.

Before the Working Session

  • Explore the RKS website and note how services, projects, people, and insights are organized.
  • Review a mix of case studies, visual essays, blogs, press, and project pages.
  • Identify language, claims, or navigation choices that feel clear, unclear, consistent, or inconsistent.
  • Note where you would expect to find files, approvals, calendars, status, and historical context.
Post-session deliverable

Prepare 5–10 questions about anything unclear, 3 observations or takeaways, and any resources or processes that were difficult to locate or understand.
